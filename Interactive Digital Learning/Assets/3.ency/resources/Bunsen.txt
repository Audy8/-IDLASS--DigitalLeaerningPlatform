WHAT IS BUNSEN BUNNER?
Named after Robert Bunsen, is a common piece of laboratory equipment that produces a single open gas flame, which is used for 
>heating
>sterilization
>combustion
The gas can be natural gas (which is mainly methane) or a liquefied petroleum gas, such as 
>propane
>butane, or a mixture of both.
